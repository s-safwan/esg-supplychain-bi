# Business Insights

## Key Findings

1. Same Day shipping records the highest average emission score.

2. Certain geographic regions consistently contribute higher average emissions than others.

3. A small number of cities account for the largest emission impact.

4. Monthly emissions demonstrate temporal variation that can support seasonal planning.

5. Higher shipment volumes do not necessarily correspond to higher average emissions.

6. Standard Class shipping shows the lowest average emission score within this estimation model.

7. Executive reporting is simplified through centralized ESG metrics.
