# Strategic Business Recommendations

Based on the ESG Analytics dashboard, the following strategic actions are recommended to the executive team:

## 1. Restructure E-Commerce Shipping Incentives
**Finding:** "Same Day" shipping is highly inefficient, generating an average emission score of 8.24 per shipment, compared to "Standard Class" at 2.09.
**Action:** Implement UI nudges and financial incentives (e.g., small discounts) at customer checkout to heavily promote "Standard Class" shipping. Shifting just 15% of Same Day volume to Standard Class will drastically reduce our global carbon footprint.

## 2. Initiate Targeted Last Mile Carrier Audits
**Finding:** A massive discrepancy exists in city-level emission efficiency.
**Action:** The Logistics Operations team must immediately audit and renegotiate with the last mile carrier vendors operating in **Pernik (15 avg score)** and **Meizhou (8.29 avg score)**, as these represent our worst performing global logistics nodes.

## 3. Reallocate Regional ESG Focus
**Finding:** High volume regions like Asian regions are actually operating at high efficiency (3.28 avg score, below global average).
**Action:** Shift sustainability oversight resources away from Eastern Asia. Redirect focus toward **Southern Africa (5.38 avg score)** and **East Africa (5.00 avg score)** to overhaul their highly inefficient logistics networks.
